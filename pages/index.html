Cloud Shop allows users to access photo and video editing software and be able to store and display their images and videos.

With this product users have a more accessible cloud-based photography and videography service that utilizes different software for editing, and organizing.

Cloud Shop also provides storage and allows users to share photos and videos that can be viewed wherever they want. Users will have a place to advertise their work.

People will be able to utilize this software to produce high-quality video and photography edits, graphics designs, etc. Users will be able to sell high-quality photos and videos online or to create covers/logos/videos for their customers or events. Cloud Shop allows users to easily log into their accounts and to collect jobs if they are so inclined.
